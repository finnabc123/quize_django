Responsible Gaming

Responsible Gaming Responsible gaming means playing a disciplined game till the point it does not affect your body and mind. Every game has a break-even point. Playing a game as per the quiz rules, and understanding when to stop playing a game is called responsible gaming.

Following are the guidelines you should follow as a safe and responsible player:

Do's:
  • You should play Quizpl with money that you can afford to lose.
  • You should decide in advance how much money you will spend playing the game and that money should not be a part of your previous game reserve.
  • You should complete all your personal and other important tasks before sitting to play so that you can play the game with a free mind.
  • You should also decide on the number of hours you will be devoting to the game.
  • You should always set deposit limits while playing so that you never deposit more than you can afford to lose.

Dont's:
  • You should not play Quizpl by taking loans.
  • You should not get into a game if you have other planned tasks on hand.
  • You should not play in a bad, sad or mad mood. This affects your game performance ultimately leading you in losing money.
  • You should take a break and pause playing for a while if you have lost money continuously 3-4 times.
