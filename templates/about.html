Who we are

We all have come across those questionnaires, click on the correct option to win while surfing the internet popup, or certain trivia games which offers rewards but are very manipulative by setting up most difficult questions in almost no time so that we end up losing a lot. In Quizpl we want you to win; hence you can play as many times and take as much time you want. Although the overall score is generated by considering both the maximum correct answers and the least amount of time the thing with us is if a question is tough, it’s tough for everyone so all you need is prior knowledge of it and swift fingers. Answer ten random questions as quickly you can, and the people with the most correct answers in the least amount of time wins the game through rankings in groups. You can play as many times as you like thus enhancing your chances to win. We want to reward people who have the knowledge but are not given due credit. First time in India, let’s learn and earn at the same time.
